A Breif History Of Nathan Pallett

My tale is a memoir stretched from the depths of deepest sorrow to the towering heights of unparralleled contentment. I was raised in a distant, mystical land known only as "Gladstone Park", overlooking the wonders of the great metropolis, Melbourne, on my horizions. A municipality that would serve as the baking tray from which my bread loaf of conquest would rise.

As a child, it was evident to my family that I was different. I wasn't the same as the other kids playing on the oval. I was gifted. Gifted with undesputible lunacy. We figured, rather than myself being thick-headed, that it was simply a sign of my advanced intellect being stagnated by my enviroment.

It wasn't.

I advanced through school with my head in my books and my books in my bag, sometimes at the same time. Despite how much I enjoyed my science and math classes, I decided against pursuing either one in university. Rather I intended to swing head first into a paying carrer in programming, accelerating my eventuall plans of domination.

Now I come to think of it, not to much extreme situations, might've oversold it a little. Either way, I'll eventually rule this city with an iron fist and claim its territory as my own. All who support me will eventually be rewarded excessivly, and all who oppose me will crumble beneath my almighty power.
This is not a joke, I WILL be your overlord.
